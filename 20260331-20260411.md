I’ve learned that language learning is not just memorizing stuff — it’s using words in real scenes, even if a scene feels difficult at first. I hope that next year I can have natural, smooth conversations with native speakers without freezing up.

I’ve learned that even the most complex projects start with solid groundwork, and I’m not afraid to start at the bottom as I shift my career direction toward AI and AI application development.
