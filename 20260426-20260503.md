## 翻译
### Nothing out of the ordinary happened these two days. I stuck to my daily routine: getting up, washing up, sending my baby to work, and then focusing on my job. Meanwhile, I kept learning new English words and phrases: concert, ex-wife, mastodon, erect, carpet, paranoid, glacier, fluff that pillow, ammunition, flatness, chaotic and twirly, ceremony, engagement, dread, stomp, perspective, presumably, sonogram.

### On the night of the 29th, my mom and little sister came to visit us. I picked them up at the station, then fetched my baby after work. After that, we went out for dinner. We had iron pot stewed chicken and fish, which tasted amazing. I also ordered some lamb skewers. We couldn’t finish all the food, so we headed home afterward.

### On the morning of the 30th, we had breakfast at the morning market. Then I took my mom and little sister to the ancient town nearby. My sister will take the high school entrance examination soon, so we went there to pray for good luck. We wandered around the town, watched performances and played games. We could win silver notes by playing games and exchange them for small gifts. I won dozens of tickets, haha, and finally got a pendant for my little sister. Later, we left the ancient town and went to eat buffet together. After the meal, we bought some pastries for my dad. In the evening, we drove back to our hometown. It was such a happy and fulfilling day!
