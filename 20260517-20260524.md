### On May 17th, I stuck to my usual routine in the daytime. In the evening, my baby and I drove back to our hometown. We bought lamb slices, green vegetables and meatballs. After arriving home, we had hot pot. We also got Sprite and a small bottle of liquor. My baby mixed drinks for me, which tasted really nice. Enjoying hot pot with drinks was such a cozy and pleasant moment.

### I got up on May 18th and worked till noon. Then I went to the supermarket to buy barbecue ingredients for our afternoon barbecue. We grilled lamb skewers that tasted great, and I’m getting more and more skilled at barbecuing. For dinner, we had tasty potato pancakes made by my mom. I also had my hair cut, then went to bed early at night.

### From the 19th to the 20th, everything went back to normal. I get up, wash up and send my baby to work every morning, then head home to work.
